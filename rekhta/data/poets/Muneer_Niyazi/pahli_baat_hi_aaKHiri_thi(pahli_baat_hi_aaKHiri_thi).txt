pahlī baat hī āḳhirī thī
is se aage baḌhī nahīñ
Darī huī koī bel thī jaise
puure ghar pe chaḌhī nahīñ
Dar hī kyā thā kah dene meñ
khul kar baat jo dil meñ thī
ās-pās koī aur nahīñ thā
shaam thī na.ī mohabbat kī
ek jhijak sī saath rahī kyuuñ
qurb kī sā.at-e-hairāñ meñ
had se aage baḌhne kī
phail ke us tak jaane kī
us ke ghar par chaḌhne kī
